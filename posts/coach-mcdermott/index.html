Is Greg McDermott the best Coach in the Big East?

College Basketball
Big East
Creighton
Author

Mark Poeckes

Published

March 21, 2023

At the end of the 2022 College Basketball season, the Big East’s premier coach Jay Wright retired following yet another trip to the Final Four. With his exit, the door is wide open for any of Wright’s foes to step up and take the crown as the best coach in the Big East. Greg McDermott has a strong case for taking that top spot with what his Creighton teams have done over the past five years. The million dollar question is, can you be the best coach in the conference despite not winning a conference championship?

One thing Greg McDermott does as well as an coach in the Nation is develop talent, we’ve seen him take small town stars, like his son Doug, and turn them into college basketball superstars. Does Greg McDermott do a better job at turning recruits into winners when comparing him to other Big East Coaches?

One of the knocks on Greg McDermott’s coaching style is that he is too fixated on his Offensive Strategy that his team’s defense generally suffers because of it. Many point to this being the reason that the Bluejay’s are never able to put together a run in March. McDermott has heard the pleas of Creighton fans and has addressed the problems on the defensive end.

Despite criticism that his team’s are “offense minded”, McDermott has proven that his Bluejay squads are consistently among the top teams in the Big East on the defensive side of the game.

To return to the million dollar question, can Greg McDermott be the best coach in the Big East despite never winning a Big East Conference Tournament. That argument should not be applied to Coach McDermott once you look at the Bluejays 2019-2020 season that was cut short due to COVID-19.

Now there are a lot of miracles that happen in March, hence the name March Madness. Nevertheless, what Greg McDermott’s Bluejays were doing in the 2020 season would have definitely made them the front runner to win the Big East Tournament. We have finally seen him break down barriers in the past couple of years by making it to the Sweet 16 and Elite Eight within three years. McDermott is eying that thrown as the best coach in the Big East.
